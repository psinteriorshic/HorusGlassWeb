From Idea to Installed Glass

1

Design & Rough Estimate

Use the online designer to choose hardware, glass thickness, and panel layout. Submit your measurements and photos for a rough estimate.

On-Site Measurement

A Horus Glass technician visits your home to take precise laser measurements and confirm all details before glass fabrication.

3

Fabrication & Scheduling

We order your custom tempered glass and coordinate installation dates directly from your account dashboard.

4

Professional Installation

Our team installs your glass, sets hardware, seals edges, and does a full quality check so everything looks and works perfectly.
